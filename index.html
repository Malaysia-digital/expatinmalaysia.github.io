About ExpatsInMalaysia

ExpatsInMalaysia is the premier resource for expatriates living in or relocating to Malaysia. Founded in 2020 by a group of long-term expats, our mission is to provide accurate, up-to-date, and comprehensive information to help international residents navigate life in Malaysia with ease.

Our team consists of experienced expats from various countries who have lived in Malaysia for years and understand the challenges and joys of expat life. We collaborate with local experts, immigration consultants, real estate professionals, and other specialists to ensure our content is reliable and practical.

Whether you're planning your move, recently arrived, or have been living in Malaysia for years, our resources are designed to enhance your experience and help you make the most of your time in this beautiful country.
